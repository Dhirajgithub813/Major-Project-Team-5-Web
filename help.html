Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Go to 'My Orders' section in your profile, select the order you want to track, and you will see the real-time status.

What is the return policy?

You can return any product within 7 days of delivery. Make sure the product is in its original condition.

How do I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before it gets dispatched from 'My Orders' page.
